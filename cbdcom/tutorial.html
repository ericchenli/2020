Call for Tutorials

In order to bridge the gap between research and real-world applications, CBDCOM’2020 solicits proposals for conventional and hands-on tutorials. Tutorial duration is two hours. Tutorials are aimed to cover topics in any of the following conference tracks or related topic:

  • Data Science & Analytics
  • Big Data Infrastructure & Management
  • Big Data Tools and Applications
  • Cloud Management & Virtualization
  • Cloud/Big Data Security, Privacy & Trust
  • Cloud/Big Data for IoT & Smart City

For more details about track topics, please consult CBDCOM’2020 CFP.

Tutorial Proposals should include:

  • Name and Affiliation of Tutor
  • Title of Tutorial
  • Tutorial Abstract (maximum of 500 words)
  • Brief Outline of the Tutorial (Showing scope of the material to be delivered and the depth to which it will be covered)
  • Target Audience
  • Importance of Tutorial to CBDCOM’2020 (Why is this topic important to CBDCOM’2020 community and what is the benefit to participants)
  • Short Bio of Tutor
  • List of venues, if similar tutorial was given in the past.

Please submit proposal to the tutorial chair, Prof. Rachid Benlamri rbenlamr@lakeheadu.ca. Early submission is encouraged and tutors will be notified within one week after submission. CBDCOM’2020 rewards each accepted tutorial with hotel accommodation for the conference dates.

IMPORTANT DATES

Tutorial Proposal Submission Due: February 1st, 2020

Tutor Notification: One week after submission date
